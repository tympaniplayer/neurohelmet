# Force generation

Beyond adding units one at a time, Neurohelmet can help you assemble and cost a whole force.

## Random force generator

Generate a force by **faction**, **era**, **size**, and **point budget**, with a **rarity bias** that
weights the draw toward more- or less-common units for that faction and era. It's a fast way to field
a themed, period-appropriate lance or company without hand-picking every unit.

## Force point budget

Set a cost limit and watch the roster total against it as you build:

- **Classic / Override** — the limit is in **BV** (Battle Value).
- **Alpha Strike / BF / SBF / ACS** — the limit is in **PV** (Point Value).

The running total updates as you add, remove, or re-skill units, so you always know how much of the
budget you've spent.

## Skill editing & re-costing

Edit a unit's skills — gunnery / piloting, driving, anti-'Mech — and Neurohelmet **re-costs** it
correctly, so the BV or PV total reflects the crew you actually fielded rather than the stock values.

## Availability lens

The catalog picker can tint and sort by **rarity / availability** for a chosen faction and era, so you
can see at a glance what a given force would realistically have access to — without hiding anything you
still want to pick.
