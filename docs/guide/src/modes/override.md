# Override

**BattleTech: Override** is a streamlined fan ruleset by
[Death From Above Wargaming](https://dfawargaming.com). Create an Override session with **`O`**.

> **Included with permission from Death From Above Wargaming.** Neurohelmet's Override support is an
> independent, non-commercial implementation of the ruleset. Find out more about BattleTech: Override
> and DFA at **[dfawargaming.com](https://dfawargaming.com)**.

## What it tracks

Any unit from the catalog is converted to an Override card and tracked live:

- **Armor & structure** — pip tracks, with the Override no-transfer rule (damage stays in the region).
- **Heat** — a 0–5 heat ladder; firing weapon groups banks heat, and end-of-turn dissipation clears it
  by the unit's sinks.
- **TIC weapon groups** — weapons packed into Target-Interval Cluster groups you fire as a unit.
- **Condition monitor** — pilot / crew hit tracking and crit summary.
- **Criticals** — per-region crit tables, available as a reference popup.

Vehicles and aerospace get their appropriate variants (vehicles shed heat; aerospace keep it and track
thrust). Override is costed in **BV**. Press **`?`** for the Override keymap.

## Peek without switching

From a **Classic** or **Alpha Strike** session you can view any unit's Override card on the fly with
**`O`**, without creating a separate Override session — handy for comparing how a unit reads across
rulesets.
