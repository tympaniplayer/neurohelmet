# Classic (Total Warfare)

The full BattleTech record sheet for a single unit. Create a Classic session with **`n`**, then add
units with `a`.

## What it tracks

- **Armor by location** — front, rear, and internal structure per location, with the standard inward
  cascade (armor → internal → transfer inward) as damage lands.
- **Heat** — the Total Warfare heat scale, with the effects a rising heat level implies.
- **Ammo** — per-bin tracking, including the consequences of an ammo hit.
- **Criticals** — the critical-slot tables per location, applied with their downstream effects.
- **Pilot / crew** — the MechWarrior condition monitor (consciousness / hit tracking).
- **To-hit & movement helpers** — a GATOR breakdown for the attack modifiers, plus movement and
  piloting-skill-roll (PSR) prompts where the rules call for one.

## Unit types

Classic handles the full spread of ground and air:

- **'Mechs** — biped, **quad**, and **tripod** configurations, each with the correct locations.
- **Combat vehicles** — including motive-system damage.
- **Infantry** — conventional infantry and Battle Armor.
- **Aerospace fighters** — armor arcs plus structural integrity.

You roll; Neurohelmet keeps the sheet and shows you what each result means. Press **`?`** for the full
Classic keymap.
